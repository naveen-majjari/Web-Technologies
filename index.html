The Game of Cricket

Introduction

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field. It is widely popular in countries like India, Australia, England, and more.

Cricket Formats

  • Test Cricket
  • One Day International (ODI)
  • Twenty20 (T20)

Interesting Facts About Cricket

Cricket originated in England and has grown into a global sport with international tournaments like the ICC World Cup.
